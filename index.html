Appliance Services

We accept service requests for the following appliance types. Availability for a specific brand or model is confirmed when your request is reviewed.

Refrigerator Repair

Service requests for cooling issues, leaks, unusual noises and other common refrigerator problems.

Washer Repair

Service requests for washing machines that won't drain, spin, fill or complete a cycle properly.

Dryer Repair

Service requests for dryers with heating problems, long cycle times or unusual noises.

Dishwasher Repair

Service requests for dishwashers that leak, don't drain, or don't clean dishes properly.

Oven Repair

Service requests for ovens with heating issues, temperature inconsistency or control problems.

Range Repair

Service requests for cooktop burners, ignition problems and range control issues.

Freezer Repair

Service requests for freezers with frost buildup, cooling problems or door seal issues.

Other Appliances

Have a different household appliance? Submit a request and we'll confirm whether we can help.

Warranty and Insurance Claims

If your appliance repair may be covered by a home warranty or insurance claim, you can include the provider name and claim number in your service request. Coverage, authorization and reimbursement depend on the terms of your individual plan.

  • Submitting a claim number does not guarantee coverage.
  • Please confirm authorization with your provider before your appointment.
  • Provider logos are not displayed here without permission.
